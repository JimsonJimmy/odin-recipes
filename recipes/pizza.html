Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular dish that originated in Italy, made with a dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of other toppings. Whether you prefer a classic Margherita pizza, a loaded meat feast, or a veggie-packed option, pizza can be customized to suit any taste. It’s a crowd-pleasing favorite for dinner or parties!

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the Topping:

  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional toppings: pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.

Steps to Make Pizza

  • Step 1: Prepare the Dough

    In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.

  • Step 2: Make the Dough

    In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms, then knead for 5–7 minutes until smooth.

  • Step 3: Let the Dough Rise

    Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.

  • Step 4: Preheat the Oven

    Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

  • Step 5: Roll Out the Dough

    Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out into a circle on a floured surface. Transfer it to a pizza stone or baking sheet.

  • Step 6: Add the Sauce and Toppings

    Spread the pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and add your choice of toppings.

  • Step 7: Bake the Pizza

    Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling and slightly browned.

  • Step 8: Slice and Serve

    Remove the pizza from the oven, slice it up, and enjoy your homemade pizza!

Muffins

Description

Muffins are small, individual-sized baked goods with a tender crumb, often enjoyed for breakfast or as a snack. These delicious treats are made with flour, sugar, eggs, milk, and butter, and can be customized with a variety of ingredients like berries, nuts, or chocolate chips. Muffins can be sweet or savory, and are perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

For the Muffins:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional: 1 cup blueberries, chocolate chips, or nuts

Steps to Make Muffins

  • Step 1: Preheat the Oven

    Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a muffin tin or line with paper muffin cups.

  • Step 2: Mix Dry Ingredients

    In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  • Step 3: Mix Wet Ingredients

    In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.

  • Step 4: Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients

    Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Avoid overmixing.

  • Step 5: Add Mix-ins

    Fold in any optional add-ins like blueberries, chocolate chips, or nuts.

  • Step 6: Fill the Muffin Tin

    Spoon the batter evenly into the muffin cups, filling each about ¾ full.

  • Step 7: Bake the Muffins

    Bake in the preheated oven for 18–20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  • Step 8: Cool and Serve

    Let the muffins cool in the tin for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely and enjoy.
